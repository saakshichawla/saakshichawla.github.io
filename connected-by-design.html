Work Experience

Design United is an optimistic platform for regional design connection and collaboration, 
that believes in creating opportunities through design in these socially distanced times.

It was founded in March 2020, by Ar. Varna Shashidhar, 
principal architect of a landscape architecture firm VSLA based in Bangalore.

The emphasis of this curated platform is to showcase fresh works and approach 
of emerging designers to highlight regional design talent 
and to create inspiration, learning, collaboration and opportunities 
that are relevant and relatable.

As a part of the core team, I have been with Design United since its inception.
My primary role is to invite, correspond and coordinate with 
the diverse set of speakers and moderators that take part in our Design Conversations.

In addition, I have been co-hosting each of the virtual webinars weekly since July this year and have moderated past events.
I also produce written and visual content, including trailer videos, feedback clips and other films related to the platform.

I find myself enriched by the exposure I have gained from this creative community
and I thoroughly enjoy working with our team at Design United.
